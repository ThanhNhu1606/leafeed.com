Green Tea Polyphenols

Green tea polyphenols are natural bioactive compounds extracted from Camellia sinensis leaves. Rich in catechins like EGCG, they offer strong antioxidant, antimicrobial, and anti-inflammatory benefits. This water-soluble, fine powder is ideal as a natural feed additive to enhance animal immunity, reduce oxidative stress, and improve gut health. It supports antibiotic-free livestock production and is suitable for pigs, poultry, cattle, and aquaculture.

  • Polyphenol content: ≥ 50%
  • Moisture content: ≤ 5%
  • Ash content: ≤ 1%
  • pH value: 4.5–6.5
  • Appearance: Green to brown fine powder

Net weight: 5kg/bag

Gross weight: 5.2kg/bag
