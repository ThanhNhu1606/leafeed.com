Vision, Mission & Business Philosophy

Our Vision

To become a pioneer in the sustainable export of green tea leaf-based feed additives, promoting diversified livelihoods for farmers, spreading green technology, and contributing to the development of clean and responsible livestock farming worldwide.

Our Mission

Understanding the importance of food safety and the concerns around antibiotic residues, we offer the ideal alternative for livestock – committed to safe, sustainable, and health-conscious supply chains. We aim to support farmers in producing reliable, clean food while enhancing animal immunity and resilience. A sustainable livestock industry begins with the right choices today.

Business Philosophy

Leafeed strives to be a trusted companion in sustainable livestock development. With a strong sense of responsibility and deep understanding of customer needs, we continuously innovate and improve product quality. We are committed to delivering effective, competitively priced solutions, upholding business ethics, and complying with all regulations.

Why Choose Us

Understanding the importance of food safety and concerns about antibiotic residues, we offer the perfect alternative in livestock farming — a solution committed to safe, sustainable, and consumer-friendly supply sources. In doing so, we aim to become a trusted partner for farmers on their journey to producing clean and reliable food. Additionally, our solution supports the enhancement of animals' natural immunity and resistance. We believe that a sustainable livestock industry begins with the right choices made today.
